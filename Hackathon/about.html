Why Cuny Yak?

Welcome to The CUNY Yak! Our idea was based off a discontinued social media app "Yik Yak", and we thought we wanted to build on the foundation of the app. Users can post comments with subject lines in the homepage, only within NYC for now. But do not worry! You can chat in the CUNY rooms as well without needing to be in NYC! This app is aimed to overall help ease stress for students to be able to speak to others in CUNY chatrooms or with users within NYC! Enjoy ranting, sharing happy news and chatting but also remember the posts disappear after 10 seconds!
